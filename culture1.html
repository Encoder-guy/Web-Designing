...FESTIVALS...

India celebrates a variety of festivals that reflect its rich cultural heritage and religions diversity.

DIWALI
Festival of Lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.
HOLI
Festival of Colours, celebrating love and the arrival of spring.
EID
Festival of Harmony, marked by prayers, fests, and charity.
CHRISTMAS
Festival of Joy, celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ.

...TRADITIONAL ATTIRE...

Indian traditional clothing varies by region, showcasing its cultural diversity:


SAREE
Elegant drape worn by women across India.
KURTA-PAJAMA
Comfortable attire for men, popular in the north.
LEHNGA
A festive outfit for women, especially in weddings.
DHOTI
Traditional men's wear in southern and eastern India.
